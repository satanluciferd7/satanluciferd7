Stay Safe Online

Cybersecurity Awareness

Protect yourself online. 500+ Nepal sites hacked in 2023. Click each topic to learn how to stay safe.

Phishing Attacks

Fake emails/SMS impersonating banks, government, or delivery services. 43% of Nepal's cybercrime.

Tip: Verify sender's email. Banks never ask passwords via email.

Malware & Ransomware

Viruses, trojans, and ransomware that lock your files and demand payment.

Tip: Use genuine antivirus. Keep backups on external drive.

Weak Passwords

Most hacked password in Nepal is '123456'. 67% of victims used weak passwords.

Tip: Use 16+ character passwords. Enable 2FA.

Public WiFi Risks

Hackers intercept data on unsecured networks. Fake hotspots common in tourist areas.

Tip: Use VPN on public WiFi. Avoid banking.

Website Security

SQL injection and XSS attacks target banking and government sites.

Tip: Check for HTTPS padlock. Report suspicious sites.

Social Engineering

Scammers exploit human psychology with fake refund calls, police calls, and job offers.

Tip: Never share OTP over phone. Report to 100 or 9851234567.
